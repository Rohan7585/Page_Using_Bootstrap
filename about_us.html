OVERVIEW

Since its inception, Aptech Computer Education Chinsurah has grown to become one of the Premier IT Software Education and Training Institutes not only in India but across 44 counties.

This achievement has been possible because of the Institute’s dedication to Premium Academic Quality, Industry Relevant Technology Learning, Assessment Systems and its Commitment to Students and Professionals.

The Academy offers a wide variety of Career, Professional, Short Term & Certification Courses along with Summer / Winter / Vocational Training & Project based training, for different segments of the IT industry. It gives great importance to providing High-Quality Education to the Students and Software Professionals enrolling with the institute for any of the courses offered.
